\chapter{Results and Discussions}\label{ch:4}

This chapter presents, analyzes, and interprets the data gathered from the respondents regarding their perception of the web-based learning tool, StatiCalcs. The responses of Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering students and instructors were analyzed using frequency distribution and weighted mean to determine their level of perception in terms of usability, accessibility, satisfaction, and preference for learning resources in studying Statics of Rigid Bodies.

The interpretation of the weighted mean was based on the five-point Likert scale presented in Chapter 3. The results are presented in tabular form together with their corresponding interpretations.
